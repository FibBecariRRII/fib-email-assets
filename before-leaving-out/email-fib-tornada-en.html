End of Mobility — Documents to Submit

Dear student,

I am reaching out because unfortunately your mobility is about to end or will end within the next month.
This is just a reminder that in order to close your mobility file, you will need to take into account the documentation to be submitted.

Documentation & Information

All the information is available on the FIB website. Please check the specific sections for each stage of your return:

Before you return →
When you return →

Certificate of Stay

It is important that the certificate of stay shows the same arrival date as the one on your arrival certificate, as this avoids inconsistencies for future audits.

Final Survey — Participant Report

You must complete the final "Participant Report" survey, which the European Union will send to you by email from the following address:

eu-corporate-notification-system@ec.europa.eu

Important: This report is completed online and is mandatory for all Erasmus+ programme participants, regardless of whether they receive a grant or not.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us. We hope your stay has been a truly enriching experience!
