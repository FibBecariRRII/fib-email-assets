End of Mobility — Action Required

Spring semester 2025–2026

Dear student,

Your academic stay is about to come to an end. Before you leave, there are a few things to take care of.

e-Secretaria

In order to have the full e-Secretaria menu available and be able to download your transcript, you must complete the RGPD Authorisations and personal data sections.

Transcript of Records (ToR)

You will be able to download your Transcript through your e-Secretaria or through our Mobility Platform by mid-July. If you download through e-Secretaria, please wait until the ECTS grade has been calculated (first week of July, approximately).

Both you and your institution will receive an email when the ToR is available on the mobility platform. Remember to use the credentials you entered when registering on the mobility platform, not your UPC credentials (those are for the e-Secretaria).

Certificate of Attendance

Send us the template of your attendance certificate to this email address a few days before you leave, and make an appointment with the mobility office to pick it up. It will only be signed on the date that you finish your mobility. Please inform us of your departure date in the email.

Important: Most students need this document, mainly Erasmus students. If you are not sure, contact your university to confirm.

Since our spring semester ends on 19/06/2026, this would be the last day we can certify, even if you decide to leave Barcelona after that date.

In case of thesis defences, the end date will be the date of your defence (thesis defences will be held until 08/07/2026).

If you have exams or academic tasks after 19/06/2026, please send us a screenshot of your Racó showing the date of the exam. If you finish your exams before that date and wish to leave earlier, the document will be signed on the date of your appointment.

Changes to the Learning Agreement

If you have made changes to your initial Learning Agreement, please send us the document of changes duly filled out and signed by you before your departure from Barcelona. We will not sign any document of changes after your mobility has ended.

Changes in OLA must also be done through the EWP platform before leaving.

If you have any doubts, don’t hesitate to contact us. We hope you have really enjoyed your stay at FIB!
